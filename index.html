About This Project

ShopSmart AI is an intelligent shopping assistant that gathers your preferences through a structured conversation, then recommends the top 3 products perfectly matched to your needs, budget, and intended use.

Tech Stack

Backend AI is powered by Flowise as the chatflow orchestrator and OpenRouter as the LLM provider.
